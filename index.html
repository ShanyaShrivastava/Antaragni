Antaragini-Fire Within Us


‘Antaragini’ is DSATM’s proud annual cultural fest that covers a range of genres from music, dance, theatre, and literary to photography, emceeing, social media content writing, and so on. ‘Antaragini’ is one of the most anticipated student-oriented cultural fests in the city, with a minimum footfall of around ten thousand students on the days of the fest. ‘Antaragni 2023’ promises to be the biggest edition of the cultural fest yet, with excitement spreading like wildfire across all social platform.
